Keeping It Local

One of the many exciting projects we were tasked with when dealing with our new expansion space, was coming up with names for our conference rooms. We decided to keep it local by naming our conference rooms after boroughs in and around Montreal!

Pictured above are only three of the eight boroughs featured in our office, but, among those, we also have Saint-Laurent, Hudson, Ville-Marie, Griffintown, and Laval.
